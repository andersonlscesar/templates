ABOUT

Marvel is a popular American entertainment company that primarily produces comic books, but also creates movies, TV shows, and other forms of media. The company was founded in 1939 by Martin Goodman as Timely Publications, and it was later renamed to Atlas Comics before becoming Marvel Comics in 1961. Marvel is known for creating many iconic superhero characters such as Spider-Man, Iron Man, the X-Men, the Avengers, the Fantastic Four, and many more. These characters have become cultural icons and have spawned a vast and interconnected fictional universe known as the Marvel Universe.

MOVIES

Marvel movies are a series of films based on characters and storylines from the Marvel Comics universe. The movies are produced by Marvel Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures. The Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) officially began with "Iron Man" in 2008 and has since become a cultural phenomenon, with each new release eagerly anticipated by fans around the world.
